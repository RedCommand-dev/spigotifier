Convert your DiamondFire games into server plugins!

DF Spigotifier is a tool to help bring your ideas to life outside of DiamondFire's limitations.

  • Input Your Template Data

    Copy the template data using
    /item nbt, or run /command
    while holding the template.
    (Requires CodeUtilities)

  • Import The Utility Classes

    Download the Utility Classes we've
    provided, and import the classes into your
    project, along with the generated code.

  • Compile Your Plugin

    Compile your plugin, and
    use it on your minecraft server!
